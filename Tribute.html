Martin Luther King Jr. (born Michael King Jr.;January 15, 1929 - April 4,1968)

Martin Luther King was an African American Baptist minister and activist who became the most visible spokesperson and leader in the American civil rights movement from 1955 until his assassination in 1968. King advanced civil rights through nonviolence and civil disobedience, inspired by his christian beleifs and the nonviolent activism of Mahatma Gandhi. He was the son of early civil rights activist Martin Luther King Sr.Mrtin Luther King Sr.
